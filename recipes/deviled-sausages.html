Deviled Sausages

Description

A fancy sausage dish, to be served over mashed potato, mashed sweet potato or steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 pound of sausages (beef or pork)
  • ½ cup of water
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tbsp tomatoe paste
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar

Steps

  1. Drizzle oil into skillet over medium heat
  2. Cook sausages in skillet, about 15 minutes, until well browned and cooked through.
  3. Drain fat, then return skillet with sausages to medium heat.
  4. Stir in water, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, cayenne pepper, tomatoe paste and brown sugar.
  5. Bring contents to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally for about 10 minutes.
  6. Serve over mashed potato (or other meal base).
