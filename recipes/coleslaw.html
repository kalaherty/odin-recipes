Coleslaw

Description

A popular cabbage based salad. This recipe is specifically meant to immitate the coleslaw found
at the chain resaurant; "KFC".

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) package of coleslaw mix (well; of course)
  • ¼ cup of finely chopped yellow onion
  • ½ cup of mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup of sugar
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp buttermilk
  • 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Stir together coleslaw mix and onion in a large bowl.
  2. Wisk together mayonnaise, sugar, milk, buttermilk, vinegar, salt and pepper in a second large bowl until smooth.
  3. Pour dressing over coleslaw mix and stir well.
  4. Chill for at least 2 hours, or overnight.
