Beef Stroganoff

Description

A meaty sauce to be served over pasta or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup of sliced fresh mushrooms
  • ⅓ cup of chopped onion
  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp seasoning salt
  • ½ tsp ground paprika
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 (10.74 ounce) can of condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • ¾ cup of sour cream, or more to taste
  • 1 tbsp of liquid smoke, or to taste (optional)
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add mushrooms and oniones and saute until the onions have turned translucent (about 5 minutes).
  3. Add ground beef and cook while strirring until browned and crumbly (5-7 minutes)
  4. Sprikle in flour, garlic powder, seasoning salt, paprika and pepper. Stir until mixed in.
  5. Add condensed soup, sour cream, liquid smoke and Worcestershire sauce.
  6. Reduce heat to medium and cook while stirring until the sauce has thickened (5-7 minutes)
  7. Serve over pasta or mashed potato.
